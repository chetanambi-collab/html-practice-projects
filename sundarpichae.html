From Engineer to CEO:The Rise of Sundar Pichai

By Chetan Ambiger|MARCH 2026



|About| Google|

Early Life and Education

Sundar Pichai was born in India and showed an early interest in technology and problem-solving.
He pursued engineering and later continued his education in the United States, building a strong academic foundation that would support his future career.

Journey at Google

He joined Google in 2004 and quickly became an important part of the company.
He worked on several key products that shaped the modern internet experience.

  • Google Chrome
  • Gmail
  • Google Drive

Rise to CEO

Due to his leadership skills and vision, Sundar Pichai was appointed CEO of Google in 2015. Later,
he also became the CEO of Alphabet Inc., managing global operations and innovation strategies.

Key Achievements

  1. Led development of Chrome
  2. Expanded Google's global reach
  3. Promoted AI and sustainability initiatives etc.
